About Logi Play

Logi Play is a lightweight browser-based hub for retro gaming and web-first emulation. Load your legally-owned ROMs right in the browser — nothing leaves your machine.

Emulator (Logi-EMU)

Fast, in-browser emulation powered by EmulatorJS. Supports GBA, SNES, NES, N64 and more — play your legally-owned games without installing extra software.

Games Directory

A future catalog for curated homebrew and free-to-play browser games. The directory will include descriptions, categories, and direct browser play when added.

Privacy & Safety

We emphasize user privacy: ROMs are loaded locally in your browser and not uploaded to our servers. Always use content you own and respect copyright rules.
