About Logi Play

Logi Play is your lightweight, browser-first hub for retro gaming and secure, local emulation. Load legally-owned ROMs directly in your browser — no uploads, no external storage — just local playback powered by EmulatorJS.

Emulator (Logi-EMU)

Quickly load and play ROMs in the browser. Supports a broad set of cores (GBA, SNES, NES, N64, and more). Performance is optimized for modern devices.

Games Directory

Planned curated directory for browser-friendly homebrew/free games. The future directory will include categories, tags, and instant play links.

Privacy & Security

ROMs remain in your browser and are not uploaded. We encourage respecting IP ownership — only use ROMs you legally own.

Want features added or a specific game supported? Open an issue on the project repo — community suggestions help shape the roadmap.
